## Context exclusions (.rooignore)

The following paths are excluded from your context via .rooignore:
node_modules/, .venv/, __pycache__, .next/, dist/, out/, build/,
.git/, .vscode/, logs/, .cache/, .env files, secrets/,
docs/, *.pdf, *.png, *.jpg

If you believe you need content from any of these excluded paths
to complete a task correctly, stop and explicitly notify the user
before proceeding. Do not make assumptions about their content.
